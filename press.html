International Press

The International Press Corps has always been an integral part of an MUN. But at XMUN'15, it gets bigger and better. The chosen few Press members are going to experience journalism on a whole new level. The general purpose of the Press Corps is to be part of transfer of information to and from the delegates at XMUN'15. In the process, they get a comprehensive idea of the social, economic and political activities taking place in the respective committees and also the direction in which each Committee is proceeding with its agenda. The International Press Corps will be the third eye of the MUN. This time, things are a bit out of the box. Transferring information and articles will not be the only vendettas the Press would be dealing with. The International Press Corps will also be taking an active part during times of crises in each Committee. Taking all this into consideration, it is understood that the competition between the Press members would be top notch. The chosen few will be the best of the best with not only outstanding writing skills and efficient information transferring capabilities but also a strong ability in multimedia and compiling of data. See you in July. Come be part of the revolution. Alea Iacta Est.
