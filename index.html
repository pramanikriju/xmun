Alea Iacta Est

July 10-12

Committees

Secretary General's Address

As Julius Caesar's army crossed the Rubicon, rising in rebellion against the state, he uttered the immortal words - "Alea iacta est".

In conceiving XMUN'15, we've trampled a few notions, only to set new benchmarks. We've ignored a few precepts, only to establish new ideas.

This July, join us at XMUN'15.

In this era of revolution, join us as we step across our Rubicon, and utter with us the immortal words, "Alea iacta est."

The die, it seems, is cast.
